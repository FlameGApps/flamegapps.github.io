About The Project
Welcome to FlameGApps, a Google Apps package for arm64 devices. We provide clean and optimized resource friendly Google Apps packages. We provide two editions - one is Basic edition which includes the core GApps like Google Play Services (GMS), Google Services Framework (GSF), Google Play Store and other essentials. The second one is Full edition, which includes all the necessary core apps with more useful Google apps like Google Dialer, Google Contacts, Google Messages, etc. We provide regular updates for all packages.
